Our Story

Tradition Meets Modernity

Founded in 2024, Lumière was born from a passion for storytelling through food. We believe that dining is not just about sustenance, but about the connection between the chef, the ingredients, and the guest.

Our philosophy is rooted in "New Classicism" – respecting traditional French techniques while embracing bold, global flavors, particularly the aromatic richness of Indian spices. We source 90% of our ingredients from local farms within 100 miles, ensuring that every dish captures the essence of the season.

Located in the heart of Bhilai, our space is designed to be an oasis of calm. The dark, moody interior, accented by warm gold lighting, creates an intimate atmosphere perfect for everything from romantic dinners to celebratory banquets.
